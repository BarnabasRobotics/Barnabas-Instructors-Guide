## Lesson Overview

In this lesson we will attach the claw assembly to the rest of the robot arm.


## Instructions

### STEP 1. Attach Claw Assembly

Attach claw to the arm using:

- 1 x M3x6 Screw (top)
- 1 x Plastic Washer
- 2 x M3x12 Screws (sides)

*IMPORTANT:* Do not overtighten.
