## Lesson Overview

In this lesson we will build and attach the claw assembly drive gear.


## Instructions

### STEP 1. Build Claw Drive Gear Assembly

Assemble claw drive gear using:

- 2 x M3x6 Screws
- 1 x Servo Horn
- 1 x Servo Screw
- 2 x Acrylic
